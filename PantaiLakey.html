About Lakey Beach

Lakey Beach is a world-class surfing destination located in Dompu Regency, West Nusa Tenggara. This beach is famous for its consistent and perfect waves suitable for surfers of all skill levels.

With a 7 km coastline facing directly to the Indian Ocean, Lakey offers some of the best surfing spots in Indonesia. Its waves are renowned for their perfect tubular shape and length, making it every surfer's dream.

Besides surfing, this beach also offers stunning views with white sand, clear blue sea water, and spectacular sunsets. The beach's natural and less crowded atmosphere makes Lakey a perfect vacation destination.

Lakey Surfing Spots

Legendary Waves

Lakey Beach has several famous surfing spots that attract surfers from all over the world. Each spot has different characteristics, suitable for various skill levels.

Lakey Peak

Best right-hand wave with long tubes

Lakey Pipe

Fast left-hand wave with perfect barrels

Nunggas

Beginner spot with gentle waves

Cobblestone

Right-hand wave with rocky bottom

Best Season

The surfing season at Lakey runs from April to October, peaking from June to August. Waves are most consistent during this period.

How to Get There

  • Fly to Sultan Muhammad Salahuddin (IATA: BMU, ICAO: WADB), in Bima
  • Land travel from airport to Dompu (about 3-4 hours)
  • From Dompu to Lakey Beach (about 30-45 minutes)

Location Details

  • Hu'u Village, Hu'u District, Dompu Regency, NTB
  • Open daily, 24 hours
  • Facing directly to the Indian Ocean
