About Satonda Lake

Satonda Lake is a volcanic lake located on Sumbawa Island, specifically in Dompu Regency, West Nusa Tenggara. This lake was formed by a volcanic eruption thousands of years ago and has the unique characteristic of having salty water despite being located inland.

The beauty of Satonda Lake lies not only in its clear blue-green water but also in the surrounding pristine scenery with green forests and cliffs encircling the lake. Visitors can enjoy natural beauty that remains largely untouched by human hands.

In addition to its natural beauty, Satonda Lake also has historical value and myths that attract tourists. According to local beliefs, the lake is considered sacred and is often used for certain traditional ceremonies.
